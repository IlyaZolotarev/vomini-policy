## 2. What Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum necessary data required to provide and maintain the app’s functionality.

### 2.1 Account Information
- **Email address** (used for login and authentication)
- If you use Google or Apple to sign in, we store only your email address — not your name or profile photo.

### 2.2 Device Information
- Device type and operating system version
- Device language/locale settings
- Push notification token (used to send updates, if enabled)

### 2.3 App Usage Data
We collect anonymous data via **Firebase Analytics**, including:
- Session duration
- Feature usage patterns
- Device model and region (based on IP, not precise location)

### 2.4 User-Generated Content
If you voluntarily use features that involve voice recording or uploading images (e.g. for study notes), we may temporarily store such content to deliver the functionality you request. We do not access microphone or camera unless you explicitly grant permission during use.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following purposes:
- To provide app services (e.g., authentication, feature access)
- To maintain app security and prevent abuse
- To send app-related notifications (only if allowed)
- To improve the app based on anonymous usage patterns

We retain data only for as long as necessary:
