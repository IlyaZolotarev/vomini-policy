You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data in the **VoMini** app.  
This page explains how to delete your account, what data is removed, and how to contact us.

---

## How to Delete Your Account in the App

1. Open the **VoMini** app.
2. Go to the **Settings** section.
3. Tap **Delete Account**.
4. Confirm the action.

Once confirmed, your account will be scheduled for deletion.

---

## Data That Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

- User profile (email and user ID).
- All dictionaries and flashcards you have created.
- Images uploaded for flashcards.
- Audio files generated during training.
- Training history and progress.
- Login tokens and session data.
- All related metadata.

This action is irreversible.

---

## Partial Data Deletion

If you want to delete specific data (categories, flashcards), you can do so manually inside the app without deleting your entire account.
